Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out — see the Resources page.

Getting Started

Who is this site for?

Current students in our learning community. It's where you can read about how we teach, find helpful resources, and log in to your own portal.

Do I need an account to use the site?

No — the Home, Philosophy, Resources, and this FAQ page are open to everyone. Only the Portal requires logging in, since it shows your own Notion page and calendar.

Account & Login

How do I get a username and password?

Accounts are set up for you — reach out if you don't have one yet or aren't sure what yours is.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Get in touch and we'll help you sort it out.

Does logging in take me straight to my Portal?

Not necessarily. Logging in from the nav takes you back to the Home page. If you were sent to the login page while trying to open the Portal, logging in takes you right back there.

Using the Site

What's in my Portal?

Your own Notion page and your calendar, each in their own tab once you're logged in.

Is the Resources page only for logged-in students?

No — it's open to anyone, no login required.
