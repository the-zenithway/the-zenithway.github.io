SAT

Official College Board Bluebook Practice Tests

College Board provides 11 Practice tests as of July 2026. These tests are the best practice materials for the test. It is basically 100% equal to how the test would look like in test date.

Khan Academy

One of the best ways to prepare for the SAT. They parternered officially with College Board, having their practice items from actual SATs.

Barron's SAT

A full-length prep book covering every section of the SAT. Includes several practice tests.

Erica Meltzer SAT Reading

The best book for SAT-level reading. Comprehensive, full of good practice questions, and well written. an unchallenged bestseller for sat reading for deserving reasons.

Erica Meltzer SAT Grammar

Haven't gone through this myself to be honest. Focused practice and strategy for SAT Writing & Language.

The Blue Book of Grammar and Punctuation

Though I think its content goes beyond the requirements for acing SAT grammar, I think there is no better book I've read that dealt confusing grammar issues better than this one.

Word Smart I

The best vocab book for SAT. Might even go beyond it.

Word Smart II

The sequel to the best selling vocab book, Word Smart.
